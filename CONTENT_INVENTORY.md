## Key Brand Messages

### Introduction Section
Shakespeare Gastropub is a modern version of traditional family place with a special warm atmosphere featuring live music, fun quiz nights, open veranda and a playground for young visitors. The restaurant's philosophy has remained consistent since 1993, emphasizing delicious cuisine, careful approach towards traditions, and first class service. The establishment combines modern and stylish presentation with authentic recipes and tastes, carefully keeping all classic cooking methods while developing skills and experimenting with taste combinations.

### What We Offer
The drinks menu includes various draft beers from around the world, exquisite wines and classic cocktails at reasonable prices. The restaurant hosts regular events including jazz nights, karaoke nights, and live entertainment for all family members. For sports enthusiasts, Shakespeare broadcasts all major sports events on multiple TV screens and two large outdoor screens.
